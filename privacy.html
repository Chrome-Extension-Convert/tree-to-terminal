Privacy Policy – Tree to Shell

1. Overview

Tree to Shell is a Chrome extension that allows users to quickly switch between a Google Doc and its corresponding Codelab preview (and vice versa). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information, browsing history, or document content.

  • Reads the URL of your active browser tab when you click its icon...
  • Fetches the codelab.json file from a Codelabs page...
  • Performs all processing locally in your browser.
